Claudia woke up from her sleep. She couldn't see anything even when her eyes were opened. The eternal darkness lied beyond her until it lost into the nothingness. As she tried to stand up, she noticed she didn't had any legs. At least, she didn't feel them. She couldn't see a glimpse of light anywhere.
As she tried to regain her consciousness slowly, she noticed that she were alone, in her own darkness. She tried to touch herself. Her own face, her own hair, her own hands. She felt completely alone...
